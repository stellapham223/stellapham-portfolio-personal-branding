## Lessons Learned

<!-- Bài học về research methodology — cách tìm tốt hơn, sources nào đáng tin -->

[2026-03-13] Video format data on LinkedIn is contradictory across sources. dataslayer.ai (Feb 2026) says video engagement is 5.60% (strong #2). postiv.ai says video reach dropped 200% vs 2024. These may measure different metrics (engagement rate vs organic reach). When advising on video, always cite both and recommend testing with one short-form video before committing.

[2026-03-13] Vietnamese LinkedIn market data is genuinely sparse online. Favikon's influencer ranking is the best source but covers only the most prominent profiles. For VN-specific posting time data, we must extrapolate from Indonesia/Singapore patterns (both GMT+7 and GMT+8). Flag this gap — first-party data from Stella's own LinkedIn analytics will be more reliable than any published study.

[2026-03-13] Shopify ecosystem is VERY well-documented (Shopify official changelog + multiple analyst sites). Always check shopify.dev/changelog and shopify.com/news for official updates before secondary sources.

[2026-03-13] PLG trend sources: productled.com (Wes Bush) is the most credible practitioner voice on PLG. userguiding.com has solid data on state of PLG. Always cross-reference both.

[2026-03-13] For VN LinkedIn thought leaders in Product/Growth: the space is largely empty at the senior practitioner level. Chip Huyen dominates VN LinkedIn in tech but her niche (AI/ML engineering) is different from Stella's. This is Stella's biggest differentiation opportunity — she can own the "Product Growth for Shopify/SaaS" niche in VN LinkedIn with almost no direct competition from local voices.
